# Customer & Revenue Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

## Objective
Analyze customer behavior and revenue performance using interactive Power BI dashboards to support data-driven business decisions.

## Key KPIs
- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Total Customers
- Average Order Value (AOV)

## Dashboard Pages

### Executive Overview
- Monthly revenue trends
- Revenue distribution by region
- Executive-level KPIs

### Customer Analytics
- Customer revenue contribution
- Orders per customer metric
- Regional customer distribution
- Key business insights

## Tools Used
- Power BI Desktop
- DAX
- Data modeling & visualization

## Key Insights
- Revenue is concentrated among a smaller group of high-value customers.
- Customer purchasing behavior varies across regions.
- The dashboard enables quick identification of revenue and customer trends.
